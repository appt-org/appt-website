<Hero
  title="Time-based Media"
  icon="/img/light/1.2.svg"
  prefix="Guideline 1.2"
/>

Everyone should have access to media, such as audio and video. People who are deaf cannot hear what is being said. People who are blind cannot see what is shown on images. By providing a transcript, subtitles and/or audio description, anyone can perceive media.
