#### January 30, 2023

Your privacy is important to us. That's why we process as little data as possible.

## Appt website

We store your preferences in Local Storage. First-party cookies are placed by [Google Analytics 4](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/12017362?hl=en). Third-party cookies are placed when you play our embedded [YouTube videos](https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies?hl=en).

### Local Storage

| Identifier | Host | Duration | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| appt:platform | appt.org | Persistent | Used to select your preferred platform in code samples |
| appt:programming-language | appt.org | Persistent | Used to select your preferred programming language in code samples |
| yt-remote-connected-devices | youtube-nocookie.com | Persistent | Used for configuration of the YouTube player |
| yt-remote-device-id | youtube-nocookie.com | Persistent | Used for configuration of the YouTube player |
| yt-player-bandaid-host | youtube-nocookie.com | Session | Used for configuration of the YouTube player |
| yt-player-bandwidth | youtube-nocookie.com | Session | Used for configuration of the YouTube player |
| yt-player-headers-readable | youtube-nocookie.com | Session | Used for configuration of the YouTube player |

### Cookies

| Identifier | Host | Duration | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| _ga | analytics.appt.org | 2 years | Used to distinguish users |
| _ga_`<container-id>` | analytics.appt.org | 2 years | Used to persist session state |
| CONSENT | youtube-nocookie.com | 2 years | Used to store consent |

## Appt app

The Appt app loads the Appt website, which means it uses the same Local Storage and Cookies. In addition, the Appt app uses [Firebase Analytics](https://firebase.google.com/docs/analytics) to collect additional information. We also use [Firebase Crashlytics](https://firebase.google.com/docs/crashlytics) to collect crash logs.

### Events

| Category | Identifier | Value | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| url | The URL of the page | - | Used to keep track of visited URL's |
| error | The URL of the page | Description of the error | Used to keep track of error messages |
